Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Introduction

MultiCalc ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculator tools.

Information We Don't Collect

Our calculators operate entirely within your browser. We do not:

  • Collect or store any personal information
  • Save your calculation results
  • Track your individual usage patterns
  • Use cookies for essential functionality

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to:

  • Serve personalized ads based on your browsing history
  • Prevent showing you the same ad multiple times
  • Measure ad effectiveness

For more information about Google AdSense, please visit Google's Advertising Policies.

Your Choices

You can control third-party advertising cookies through your browser settings. To opt out of personalized advertising by Google, visit Google Ad Settings.

Data Security

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere online.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page.
